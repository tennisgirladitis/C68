Stem Mentorship and Fair

Learn how to make a science experiment and present it in the science fair

The STEM Fair is an event where students can showcase science and engineering projects. Prior to the event, students find a question or problem that interests them. They build prototypes or conduct experiments, sharing what they learned with their peers. Students may win awards based on the quality of their projects and presentations. The STEM Fair will be held at the Rochester Regional Sports Complex at RCTC on Friday, March 1st, 2024. This will be a non-school day for Rochester Public Schools.

Welch Village Skiing

learn how to ski

Every year, almost 400 students from Rochester and the surrounding area travel to Welch Village Ski & Snowboard Area as part of Rochester Community Education Ski Club. Students who register for Ski Club receive coach bus transportation to and from Welch Village. Many students tell us that Ski Club is one of their favorite parts of the school year and that they love the freedom to explore Welch Village with their friends. Ski Club does not include formal lessons. Students are expected to choose runs that are appropriate for their experience level. Prior to the start of Ski Club, your school's Ski Club Advisor will reach out with more information.

School Achievements

2023 SEMYO Award Recipient
Congratulations Dr. Rusin!

SEMYO is pleased to announce that John Adams Middle School Orchestra Director Dr. Sarah Rusin is this year’s recipient of the Bravura Award for Inspiring Excellence in Music Education. Dr. Rusin was nominated for her musical expertise as well as her kindness, positivity, and willingness to go above and beyond to meet her students’ needs. Dr. Sarah Rusin has taught orchestra in the Rochester Public Schools for ten years: first at John Marshall High School and John Adams Middle School, and now solely at John Adams where she teaches full time after growing the program. In addition to teaching orchestra at John Adams, she plays viola in the Rochester Symphony.
